Drawing App

I created a drawing app using p5.

left arrow key= Bigger line
right arrow key= Smaller line
B= Black
W= White
R= Bordeau
Y= Yellow
G= Teal
O= Orange
S= Save Canvas
Middle Mouse Key= square
backspace= Erase Canvas
