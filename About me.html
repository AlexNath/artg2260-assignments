About Me:
Hi, I am a fourth year Game Design student, my interest include reading, watching movies, bouldering and playing games.
The main reason for which I wanted to take this course is that, when designing a game, it is a must to take into account what can and cannot be done. In order for me to fully comprehend how someone could program the game I am working on I have to learn how programming works.
My personal interest in games is the balance between the mechanics, the art and the story. Because while there are great games the rely mostly on mechanics, the most memorable ones definitely seem to find a balance between all, which is what makes them memorable.
